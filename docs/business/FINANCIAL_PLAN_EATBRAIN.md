**Author:** Senior Producer & Rust Systems Architect

**Status:** Internal Planning

## 1. Value Proposition (The "Neurofunk" Angle)
Eatbrain isn't just a label; it's a technical standard in drum & bass. Nullherz provides them with a **proprietary performance platform** that matches their aesthetic: dark, high-precision, and technically superior.

### 1.1 Why Eatbrain Should Pay
*   **Exclusive Tech:** A unique engine that no other label (RAM, Hospital, Critical) has access to.
*   **Artist Synergies:** The Sampler and Sequencer are perfect for the complex rhythmic structures of neurofunk.
*   **Branded Hardware Potential:** Licensing the engine for a "Limited Edition Eatbrain Controller/Groovebox."

## 2. Proposed Pricing Models

### Model A: Custom Engine Development (Tier 1 - The "Full Buildout")
*Best if they want to own/exclusively use the tech for a fixed period.*
*   **Amount:** **€80,000 – €120,000**
*   **Structure:**
    *   30% Upfront (Commencement)
    *   40% Milestone 1 (Control Plane & MIDI)
    *   30% Final Delivery (Alpha Release)
*   **Inclusions:** 3 months of dedicated dev (as per roadmap), exclusive branding, and 1-year maintenance.

### Model B: Strategic Partnership & Licensing (Tier 2 - The "Collaborative" Route)
*Best for long-term recurring revenue.*
*   **Amount:** **€25,000 – €40,000 (Initial Setup) + 15% Revenue Share** on branded sales.
*   **Structure:** Lower upfront cost, but we share in the success of the "Eatbrain Edition" software or hardware.

### Model C: R&D Sponsorship (Tier 3 - The "Label Support" Route)
*Best for getting the project to alpha without losing IP.*
*   **Amount:** **€5,000 – €8,000 per month** (Retainer)
*   **Duration:** 6 months.
*   **Benefit:** They get early access for their top-tier artists (Burr Oak, MNDSCP) to test and influence the feature set.

## 3. Negotiation Strategy

### 3.1 Positioning your "AI-Driven" Efficiency
Do not pitch yourself as "one guy." Pitch yourself as a **"Single-Developer Force Multiplier."**
*   **The Line:** "I use advanced AI orchestration to deliver the output of a 10-person engineering team. You aren't paying for my hours; you are paying for the technical moat I am building for your label."

### 3.2 The "Neuro" Comparison
Remind them of the cost of legacy systems. A custom C++ engine would cost them 3x more and take 2x as long. Nullherz is faster, safer, and already operational.
