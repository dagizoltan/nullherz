*   **Artist Synergies:** The Sampler and Sequencer are perfect for the complex rhythmic structures of neurofunk.

> **Evidence alignment (July 18, 2026)** — before this document goes in front of a counterparty, two claims above must be softened to match the [R&D Strategy](./R_AND_D_STRATEGY.md) status labels: "neural timbral embeddings" are currently a schema + placeholder projection (real 16-D latent pipeline, not yet neural), and RDMA is parked research (shipped: measured-path-delay clock sync + remote sidecar DSP over UDP — itself genuinely unique, see [Market Comparison §6](./MARKET_COMPARISON.md)). What has *strengthened* since June and belongs in the pitch: 157-test verified engine, CI + weekly formal proofs, crash-isolation test-pinned, signed-DNA security shipped, and a survival/latency harness producing publishable numbers. Pitch the verified engine hard and the R&D as roadmap — not the reverse.

## 2. Proposed Pricing Models

### Model A: Proprietary IP & Engine Development (Tier 1 - The "Technical Moat")
*Best if they want to own/exclusively use the tech for a fixed period.*
*   **Amount:** **€150,000 – €250,000**
*   **Structure:**
    *   30% Upfront (Commencement)
    *   40% Milestone 1 (Control Plane & MIDI)
    *   30% Final Delivery (Alpha Release)
*   **Inclusions:** 3 months of dedicated dev (as per roadmap), exclusive branding, and 1-year maintenance.

### Model B: Strategic Partnership & Licensing (Tier 2 - The "Collaborative" Route)
*Best for long-term recurring revenue.*
*   **Amount:** **€25,000 – €40,000 (Initial Setup) + 15% Revenue Share** on branded sales.
*   **Structure:** Lower upfront cost, but we share in the success of the "Eatbrain Edition" software or hardware.

### Model C: R&D Sponsorship (Tier 3 - The "Label Support" Route)
*Best for getting the project to alpha without losing IP.*
*   **Amount:** **€5,000 – €8,000 per month** (Retainer)
*   **Duration:** 6 months.
*   **Benefit:** They get early access for their top-tier artists (Burr Oak, MNDSCP) to test and influence the feature set.
